About us

Our comprehensive service offers foreign purchase.
Our products created the ‘last mile solution’ for commuting:
the concept of dividing commuting into two stages,
whereby long distances are covered by traditional transportation such as trains and cars,
and crowded urban environments are navigated via personal mobility tools such as electric scooters.

MISSION:

To reduce car consumption in urban environments.
To offer an alternative solution of personal light transportation for short distances.
One that saves time and is green, effective, sweat-free and fun.
